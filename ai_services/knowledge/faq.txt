FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What time is breakfast served?
A: Breakfast is served from 6:30am to 10:30am in our main dining room.

Q: Is Wi-Fi included?
A: Yes, free Wi-Fi is included in all room rates.

Q: Can I extend my stay?
A: Yes, subject to availability. Please contact the front desk.

Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: Cancellations must be made 24 hours before check-in for a full refund.

Q: Do you allow pets?
A: Yes, pets are allowed with a $50 fee per night.

Q: Is there parking available?
A: Yes, free parking is available for all guests.

Q: Can I request a late checkout?
A: Yes, late checkout is available for $50 per hour, subject to availability.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and cash.

Q: Is the fitness center open 24 hours?
A: Yes, the fitness center is open 24 hours for guests.

Q: Do you have a business center?
A: Yes, a fully equipped business center is available for guest use.
